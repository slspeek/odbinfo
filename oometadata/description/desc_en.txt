Creates a html-report of the components of a LibreOffice database.
The tables, views, queries, forms, reports, macros and mail-merge
documents get their own detailed page. Their dependency relations
are hyperlinked.
The report contains a diagram with all the components as nodes
and their relations as arrows.

Please see: https://github.com/slspeek/odbinfo#installation on how
to install and use this tool.
